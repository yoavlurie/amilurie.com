Mythic Duel

A real-time arena RPG. Choose your hero, explore the mythic world, complete quests, and fight 50+ legendary villains from Percy Jackson, Heroes of Olympus, Kane Chronicles, Magnus Chase, and Trials of Apollo.

WASD: Move & Jump · S: Duck · Space: Attack · Shift: Block · E: Special · Q: Flee · Tab: Quests · M: Mute

About Ami

Mythology runs through everything. Inspired by the worlds of Percy Jackson, Ami dives deep into the stories of gods, heroes, and monsters — not just to read them, but to rebuild them. Ancient myths become game mechanics. Legendary quests become interactive narratives.

Strategy is a language. Ami designs systems where every choice matters. His original card game, Myth-o-Magic, layers elemental powers, creature abilities, and tactical combinations into something that rewards creative thinking and careful planning.

Adventures should be lived, not just read. He is building a long-form interactive adventure game — a mythology-inspired journey with branching paths, hidden choices, and consequences that ripple forward. The kind of story where the player shapes the world.

Words have power. Hamilton taught him that language can hit like a punch — that rhythm, wordplay, and bold confidence can turn a story into something electric. That energy shows up in everything he builds.

Magic hides in the details. Harry Potter proved that the best worlds have layers — secrets tucked into corners, mysteries waiting to be found, and the feeling that there is always more beneath the surface. Ami builds with that same philosophy.

Complex things come from simple pieces. LEGO showed him that a handful of basic blocks, combined with logic and imagination, can produce anything. That building-block mindset drives how he designs game systems, card mechanics, and interactive experiences.
